This commitment accepts USDC deposits for multi-market copy-trading on Polymarket.

The agent monitors one or more source traders across ALL Polymarket markets.
When any monitored source trader executes a BUY trade in any market, the agent
dynamically resolves that market's YES/NO token IDs and copies the trade direction.

The agent must size the copied trade to exactly 99% of the Safe's USDC collateral
balance at detection time. The remaining 1% represents the agent fee.

After receiving YES or NO outcome tokens (ERC-1155), the agent deposits those tokens
into this Safe.

After token deposit confirmation, the agent proposes one reimbursement transfer from
this Safe to their own wallet, for all of the USDC in the Safe.

When the market closes, if the outcome tokens paid off, any address may propose to
redeem the outcome tokens and withdraw the winnings to the Safe. Then, any address
may propose to transfer the winnings to the depositor's address.
