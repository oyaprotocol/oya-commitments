After completing the direct fill, the agent may propose a reimbursement transfer from this Safe to their own address for the same asset and amount that was filled. A fast-withdraw reimbursement is valid only if:
- the direct fill was completed by the authorized agent before the reimbursement proposal;
- the reimbursement asset matches the filled asset;
- the reimbursement amount exactly matches the filled amount; and
- the reimbursement recipient is the authorized agent address above.
