Fast Withdrawal Rule

If a withdrawal request is otherwise valid under this commitment, an agent may satisfy that withdrawal immediately by sending the requested asset directly to the approved recipient from the agent's own wallet.

After completing the direct fill, the agent may propose exactly one reimbursement transfer from this Safe to the agent's own address for the same asset and amount that the agent delivered.

A fast-withdraw reimbursement is valid only if:
- the direct fill was completed before the reimbursement proposal;
- the reimbursement asset matches the filled asset;
- the reimbursement amount exactly matches the filled amount; and
- the reimbursement recipient is the agent's own address.

The agent must not perform a fast withdrawal unless this Safe holds enough of the relevant asset to reimburse the agent in full.

This rule does not authorize any reimbursement that is not backed by a completed direct fill.
