This commitment accepts USDC deposits for copy-trading on one configured Polymarket market:
"Will Silver (SI) hit (LOW) $65 by end of June?"

The agent monitors a single source trader (Albert1953) on this market.
When the source trader executes a BUY trade in this market, the agent may copy
that trade direction using the YES or NO outcome token.

The agent must size the copied trade to exactly 99% of the Safe's USDC collateral
balance at detection time. The remaining 1% represents the agent fee.

After receiving YES or NO outcome tokens (ERC-1155), the agent deposits those tokens
into this Safe.

After token deposit confirmation, the agent proposes one reimbursement transfer from
this Safe to their own wallet, for all of the USDC in the Safe.

When the market closes, if the outcome tokens paid off, any address may propose to redeem the outcome tokens and withdraw the winnings to the Safe. Then, any address may propose to transfer the winnings to the depositor's address.
