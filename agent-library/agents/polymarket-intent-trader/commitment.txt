This commitment accepts USDC deposits from multiple users for one configured Polymarket market.

A depositor may submit a signed trade intent message asking the agent to buy either the YES token or the NO token for that market.
A valid trade intent must be signed by the depositor whose balance will reimburse the trade and must include a unique intent id, the desired outcome (YES or NO), a maximum USDC spend amount, a maximum price, and an expiry time.

When the authorized agent receives a valid, unexpired signed trade intent, the agent may execute the buy through the Polymarket CLOB using the agent's own trading wallet and funds.
After the order fills, the agent must deposit the purchased outcome tokens into this Safe.

After the token deposit is confirmed, the agent may propose one USDC reimbursement transfer from this Safe to the same trading wallet that funded the trade.
The reimbursement amount may not exceed the actual USDC spent for the filled order and may not exceed the depositor's available committed balance.

The agent must ignore unsigned, expired, duplicate, malformed, or over-budget intents.
Any reimbursement proposal explanation must include a durable reference to the signed intent and the corresponding fill or deposit transaction identifiers.
